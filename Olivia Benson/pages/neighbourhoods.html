Neighbourhoods

Do you want to hear some good news? The latest trend with estate living in Johannesburg is that residential offerings are no longer reserved for the top 1% of society.

The Johannesburg South Neighbourhood includes the suburbs of Bassonia, Glenvista, Lenasia, Oakdene, Winchester Hills and Mulbarton. Although the area is being developed fast, Johannesburg South still retains much of its natural beauty. With the endless hills disappearing into the horizon, and many exclusive wildlife estates, one might imagine being in a nature reserve. "The South", as it is most commonly referred to by the locals, is mainly divided into two main parts - the older part on the north side of the N12, and the newer part on the south of the highway. The people of "the South" are family-orientated and active.

Its extremely scenic, and there are quite a number of good restaurants. The people of “The South” are considered to be very health conscious - so a lot of fitness facilities including gyms, clubs in terms of cycling clubs, running clubs, pilates and yoga clubs are available.

Above and beyond the safety and security offered by estate living in Johannesburg, it has become a welcoming addition to many lifestyles. From golfers to eco-living, there's an estate in the city's boundaries that's set to cater to your needs. The latest trend with estate living in Johannesburg is that these residential offerings are no longer reserved for the top 1% of society. Recent developments have brought highly affordable cluster and apartment offerings within these gated communities, offering residents all the facilities of the lifestyle estate but within reach of a young professional's budget.

Estate living in Johannesburg has become more affordable in recent years with many of these estates adding apartment development to their plates. Looking at the success of The Village at the Eye Of Africa Estate in the south of Johannesburg, which comprises of 12 apartment blocks, with eight two- or three-bedroom apartments in each block. Sales have gone exceptionally well. Within weeks of Phase 1 of the launch, 80% of the first apartment block was sold.

Eye of Africa Residential Estate

Set in the lush surroundings of the pristine Eye of Africa, the stands, apartments, cluster and luxury homes available offer the very best in contemporary modern living. Fully customisable to each individual owners taste, these breathtaking new properties have been specifically conceptualised to embody attractive, low maintenance, lock-up-and-go living. Eye Of Africa is the epitome of estate living in Johannesburg with its beautiful views and relaxed atmosphere.

Meyersdal Nature Estate

Just south of the city centre lies the 300ha Meyersdal Nature Estate, offering a safe and secure environment on 272 large stands - ranging from 900m² to 2000m². With its walking and mountain bike trails, Meyersdal Nature Estate affords nature lovers close contact with the natural environment and creates a lifestyle that enhances the relationship between man and nature while preserving and protecting endangered fauna and flora species of the larger 1080ha nature area. Living amongst wildlife in a true nature estate is a lifestyle dream for residents supporting a philosophy whereby man lives in harmony with nature.
