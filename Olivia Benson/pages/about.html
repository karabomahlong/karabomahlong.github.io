Get to know more about me.

I strive to demonstrate the highest level of integrity and commitment, and to bring a little fun into every real estate transaction. I've lived here all my life and I love Johannesburg as much as I love my job!

As the trusted real estate advisor for my clients, I understand that selling and/or buying a home is complex and deeply personal. There is also a lot at stake financially and personally. This perspective on the process and the responsibility felt towards my clients drives me to truly be an expert at the job. I understand the market, the players, and all of the variables involved in a real estate transaction. I want to make sure you feel supported and that you have a trusted advisor by your side.

Humble Beginnings. In 2015, I launched my real estate career by creating and filming Johannesburg's first real estate Day in The Life documentary series about the ins and out of the real estate market.

My reputation amongst my peers is as a well-respected industry innovator and early adopter of social media and digital marketing for real estate. My team and I work hard to keep ahead of the curve, combining luxury home styling with technology and forward-thinking. I've spent the past 6 years excelling in the competitive landscape of Johannesburg real estate and worked behind the scenes with hundreds of other real estate agents.

More about me. I graduated from Jeppe High School for Girls in Kensington, and at UNISA with a degree in Public Relations and Communication. It was a well-rounded education that taught me to think critically. Side note: I was the captain of our women's rugby team too and played “second row”.

My secret sauce. The reason for the 5 star client reviews, is directness, transparency, stylish home staging and compelling listing marketing, expert negotiatons, and the ability to keep people and deals on track — calmly and professionally. This ability is part natural instinct and part commitment to advanced, ongoing training and education. A lot of what agents do sound similar on paper. What varies (significantly) is how they do it and the resulting outcomes for their clients.

I've renovated homes, worked on development projects, and staged, marketed and sold over 500 homes for my clients. As an aspiring flâneuse, I am a good person to ask for food, wine, shopping and travel tips. In fact, I can't wait to take you out to celebrate after the close of our sale!

In terms of real estate, my team and I are committed to lifelong learning. Our education is never complete. As a licensed Broker (as opposed to Salesperson), I continually invest time, money and thought into the latest sales, marketing and especially, negotiations techniques. Everyone on our team is smart, educated, experienced and dedicated to continual improvement.

On a personal note, I care very deply about my client's individual situation, goals, and dreams. Johannesburg real estate is exciting, challenging and often exhilarating. It's not for the faint of heart!

If you'd like to work together, please reach out. I look forward to getting to know you and i'm here to help achieve your goal.
