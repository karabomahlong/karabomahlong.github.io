When you make a move, you want to sell your house for the highest price possible and working with an agent to set the right asking price is critical, as pricing it too high or too low could have a negative impact on your final sale.

Before anything else, it's a good idea to sit down and clarify your motivations and draw up a basic time frame for the selling process. Being clear about your intentions for selling will make it easier for us to determine the most appropriate option for your specified financial lifestyle, and real estate goals.

I'll then visit your property and give you an accurate comparative market analysis to establish the fair market value of your home based on factors like your property's features and recent sales activity in your neighbourhood. Then together we'll decide on the best selling price. Furthermore, recommendations of any potential improvements or staging to maximise your home's value will be made.

While everyone has clutter, buyers want to see a fantasy version of your house, in which they can envision living. Once your home is on the market you'll need to keep it as neat as possible. One way to make that easier is to reduce the amount of clutter you have on your shelves and surfaces.

Improving your home in preparation for viewing is essential. While you don't necessarily want to do a major, expensive renovation project before you sell, you can make minor repairs and improvements that will make your home look fresher to buyers. Try things such as replacing the caulk and grout in your bathroom, updating old or rusted ceiling fans and light fixtures, and changing switch plates, doorknobs and other hardware for a clean and neat appearance. Consider painting your front door and trim even if your rooms don't need new paint.

Having a detailed marketing plan will make a big difference in how quickly your home sells and how much of a profit you'll realise.

    Marketing
  • Your home will be professionally photographed and a video property tour will be made so that local and especially international buyers can get a better feel for the home before they view the home in person.
  • Just Listed flyers will be distributed to your neighbourhoods.
  • Your home will be advertised on all major local property websites (Property 24, Private Property, etc) as well as direct marketing to a pool of exclusive buyers.
  • Open houses will be scheduled and held at your convenience.

However If you plan to buy another home, an important decision to make is whether to sell your home first or make an offer on a new home before putting yours on the market. I can help you evaluate how fast homes are selling in the market and help you estimate how long it will take you to find a home. This decision also depends on your financing, so you may want to consult with a lender to see how you can finance the transition from one home to another if you choose not to sell your home first.
